What is Retake?

Retake is a cross-platform esports companion that brings live Counter-Strike scores to your phone, tablet, and browser. Whether you're on the go or at your desk, you get instant access to match results, player performance, and the tournaments that matter.

  • Filter matches by Yesterday / Today / Upcoming
  • Search and follow your favorite teams
  • Rich match detail modals with round and team breakdowns
  • System-aware dark / light theme
  • iOS and Web from a single codebase
